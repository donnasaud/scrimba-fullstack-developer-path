Visit Jeddah:

Where the Red Sea Meets History.

Top four activities to do at Jeddah

Historic Al-Balad

A UNESCO World Heritage Site filled with coral houses, winding alleys, and traditional markets.

King Fahd's Fountain

The tallest fountain in the world, a Jeddah icon reaching over 300 meters high.

Islamic Arts Biennale 2025

A global showcase of Islamic arts, with 30+ institutions and 29 new commissions by artists from the region and beyond.

Formula 1 Saudi Arabian GP

Experience elite racing and electrifying entertainment on the iconic Jeddah Corniche Circuit. More than a race, it's a celebration of speed, energy, and modern Saudi Arabia.
